    Affected By Covid-19? Now What Next?
  1. Wash your hands frequently and carefully
  2. Avoid touching your face
  3. Do not share personal items
  4. Stop shaking hands and hugging people
  5. Cover your mouth and nose when you cough and sneeze
  6. Clean and disinfect surfaces
  7. Take physical (social) distancing seriously

    Affected By Heart Disease? Now What Next?
  1. Quit smoking
  2. Ditch unhealthy foods
  3. Commit to a heart-healthy eating style
  4. Exercise regularly
  5. Limit alcohol intake
  6. Control your weight
  7. Reduce chronic stress
